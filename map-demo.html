Firestorm – Map Demonstration

As another example of what can be done with Firestorm, see http://www.sanbachs.net/cgi-bin/mexico/map-demo.cgi/Cities=MEXCUNACA.

In the Location: area of your browser, modify this URL to replace "MEXCUNACA" with any number of three letter city codes chosen from the list below. A map will be computed showing the selected cities, with links to each, based on data stored in a DP database. Some reflection will show that there are 2 to the twenty-first power or approximately two million possible maps. 2 21 = 2,097,152.

ACA
Acapulco
CDV
Ciudad Victoria
CHI
Chihuahua
COZ
Cozumel
CUN
Cancun
GDL
Guadalajara
LAP
La Paz

León
MAN

MEX

MID

MTY

MZT

OAX

PUE
Puebla
PVR

SAL

TAM

TIJ

TUX

VER

The possible maps range from one with no cities at all to one with all of the twenty-one cities.
